What is Sludge Reducing Bacteria?

Sludge Reducing Bacteria (SRB) & Sludge Reducing Bacteria MAX (SRB MAX) are unique blends of Bacillus bacteria mixed with more than 100 micro-nutrients. This blend of bacteria and micro-nutrients is scientifically proven to digest sludge & reduce fats, oils, grease, odors, & Hydrogen Sulfide (H2S).

What is Sludge Reducing Bacteria?

To apply “SRB” and “SRB MAX,” simply toss the water-soluble packets into the lagoons or treatment basin. Concentrate the applications of bacteria in areas where sludge accumulation is greatest. This typically includes; the inlet, side slopes, corners, and other targeted spots. These certified non-pathogenic bacteria will work effectively to digest the sludge and improve overall treatment.

An alternative method for applying “SRB” and “SRB MAX” involves pre-hydrating the product for 1-4 hours with water, then mixing the bacteria solution with a cordless drill and mixing adapter. To apply the solution, use a cup or another delivery method. For optimal distribution, pour the liquid solution off the back of a boat near the outboard motor propeller. It is recommended to maintain a minimum dilution ratio of 4 gallons per 1-2 packets.

Application Instructions & Product Descriptions for SRB & SRB MAX

Initial Dose

The “initial dose” is based on the volume of the lagoons. Apply the product evenly, ensuring that any additional product is applied to areas where sludge accumulation is greatest.

Ongoing Dose

The “ongoing dose” is based on the average daily flow. Apply the product evenly, ensuring that any additional product is applied to areas where sludge accumulation is greatest.

Additional Products for Consideration

Liquid Sludge Reducing Bacteria

“SRB” is available in a liquid form (LSRB). Substitue 1 Gallon of LSRB for every 4.2 packets of “SRB”. Available in 5, 55, & 275 gallons.

Liquid Sludge Reducing Bacteria Cold

“SRBC” is available in a liquid form (LSRBC). Substitue 1 Gallon of LSRBC for every 4.2 packets of “SRBC”. Available in 5, 55, & 275 gallons.

Liquid Injection Peristaltic Pump

Automate your liquid injection system with the addition of a peristaltic pump. 110-120 volt single phase electricity is required.

Sludge Reducing Bacteria DARTS

SRB “DARTS” are a speacialized "slow-releasing" formulation of SRB. DARTS are perfect for “sludge mounds” or any areas with significant sludge accumulation. Each DART is 6.5 oz. Available in a 30 pound pail.

Block of SRB

Block of SRB is a a specialized "slow-releasing" formulation of SRB that dissolve slowly over 30 days. Block of SRB is perfect for lift stations, wet wells, and collection systems. Available in 5, 10, & 20 pound blocks.

Liquid & Air Distribution System

The Liquid & Air Ditribution System is a 12-volt battery operated system for use on a boat. Inject air and bacteria directly into the sludge layer. This system is ideal for evenly distributing any product to very large lagoons. Liquid & Air Distribution System
